Five Aggregates v3 Skandha Wheel — Moment of Consciousness

  1. Select an aggregate tab below the wheels — Form, Feeling, Perception, Mental Formations, or Consciousness
  2. Each wheel has 9 slices grouped into three zones: pleasant, neutral, and unpleasant
  3. Label each slice with a word that describes a state of that aggregate. You may use the example words provided — tap show examples to see them. Examples fill slices in order — to place a word in a specific slice, type it directly into any slice box instead.
  4. To correct a label: click into any filled box and retype or delete
  5. To start a wheel over: tap Clear all slices at the bottom of that tab
  6. Once your slices are labeled, drag any wheel to spin it — fast or slow, either direction
  7. The glowing slice pointing inward toward the center shows your current state for that aggregate

Your labels and wheel positions are saved automatically in your browser and will be here when you return. Note: clearing your browser data or opening this on a different device will reset your labels.
